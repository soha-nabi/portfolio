Fuel Amigo is an innovative platform developed to simplify fuel delivery and management for individuals and businesses. Designed with a focus on real-time tracking, safety, and convenience, it enables users to request fuel directly to their location through a seamless interface. The project won multiple hackathons for its innovation, usability, and potential to revolutionize urban fuel logistics.
